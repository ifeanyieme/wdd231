Community Facts

  • Population: 3.5 million (Abia State)
  • Founded: 1991 (Abia State) / Ancient History (Aba and Umuahia)
  • Key Industries: Agriculture, Oil & Gas, Textiles, Manufacturing, Trade
  • Annual Visitors: Over 150,000 (mainly for trade, business, and cultural events)
  • Average Household Income: ₦600,000 annually (approx.)
  • Notable Landmarks: National War Museum (Umuahia), Aba Arts & Crafts Market, Azumini Blue River
  • Public Services: 24/7 Health Clinics, Abia State Library, Abia State Polytechnic
  • Public Transportation: Bus Services, Taxis, Tricycles (Keke NAPEP), Motorcycles (Okada)
  • Annual Events: Abia State Trade Fair, New Yam Festival, Aba International Trade Fair
  • Climate: Tropical Climate with a Wet Season (May–October), Dry Season (November–April), Average Temperature: 27°C (81°F)

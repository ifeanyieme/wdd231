Site Plan: Cycling & Swimming Enthusiasts Hub

Site Name

Site Name: Cycling & Swimming Enthusiasts Hub

This name was chosen to reflect the dual-focus community and content: cycling and swimming. It is straightforward, searchable, and inclusive of a broad audience.

Optional domain: cycle-swimhub.com

Site Purpose

This website will serve as a comprehensive hub for individuals who are passionate about cycling and swimming. It will offer:

  • Training tips and video guides for both sports.
  • Gear reviews for bikes, swimwear, and accessories.
  • Event listings for races and local meetups.
  • Health and fitness advice tailored to these activities.
  • A community forum to share experiences, ask questions, and connect with others.

Scenarios

  • What type of swimwear or cycling gear is best for beginners?
  • Where can I find upcoming swimming and cycling events near me?
  • How can I improve my endurance for triathlon training?

Color Schema

  • Primary Color: #1e88e5 – used for headings, links, and accents.
  • Secondary Color: #e3f2fd – used for background sections and callouts.

Typography

  • Headings: Montserrat, sans-serif – used for all major headings (h1-h3).
  • Body Text: Open Sans, sans-serif – used for paragraphs and regular content.
